# Doctor Appointment & Health Record System

## Project Idea
A web-based platform that allows patients to book doctor appointments, store medical records, and receive prescriptions digitally. Doctors can manage schedules and access patient history, while admins manage users and system operations.

## Scope
- Patient appointment booking
- Doctor schedule management
- Prescription uploads
- Secure medical record storage
- Role-based access (Patient / Doctor / Admin)

## Key Features
- Appointment calendar
- Digital prescriptions
- Medical history tracking
- Secure login and authorization

## Advanced Features
- AI symptom checker
- Health analytics dashboard

## Real-world Use
Applicable for clinics, hospitals, and telemedicine platforms.
